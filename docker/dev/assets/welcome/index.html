Welcome to the Senior Common Room

Use the arrow keys to navigate around the rooms

When you are at a location where you can interact with something or somebody, then a message appears at the bottom of the screen. Press either the Enter or Space key to activate the action. Alternatively you can also click on the action message to activate the action.

Most actions open some kind of additional interface. To close this, click on the "x" in the top-right corner or click outside of the additional interface.
